A few notes

No new account

Sign in with GitHub.

Your friends already have one. No new password, no email confirmation loop.

Private by group

Only members see it.

Each group is invite-only. Nothing is public, nothing is indexed.

Installable

Works as an app.

Add to your home screen on iOS or Android. Works offline once loaded.

Open source

The math is auditable.

The whole client is on GitHub. Inspect the splits, fork it, host your own.
